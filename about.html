About Cashibra

I'm 12 years old and I noticed no one was teaching kids about real money.

So I created Cashibra ‚Äî a fun platform where learning about money, credit, taxes, and rewards is gamified for kids and teens aged 10 to 19.

With Cashibra, lessons are interactive, challenges feel like games, and every point earned teaches a real financial skill. By age 16, players unlock a virtual credit card system to practice real-life credit management safely.

Why Cashibra Exists

I realized that most young people grow up with almost zero financial knowledge. Schools rarely teach interest, taxes, or credit scores. Cashibra fills that gap, making financial literacy fun and rewarding.

Our Mission
