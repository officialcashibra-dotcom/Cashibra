About Cashibra

Cashibra is a platform created to help kids and teens understand money in a fun and simple way. We believe financial education should start early, and our mission is to make it accessible for ages 10–19.

We break down money topics like saving, spending, budgeting, digital payments, taxes, credit score, and more — into easy lessons and interactive games.

Created by a young developer, Cashibra is still growing and in development. More lessons, interactive tools, and features will keep being added.
