Needs vs Wants

Understanding the difference between NEEDS and WANTS is the first step to becoming money-smart.

Needs = What you MUST have to survive.

Wants = Things you LIKE but don't NEED.

“If you buy all your WANTS, you’ll never have money for your NEEDS.”

“The quickest way to save money: delay your wants.”
