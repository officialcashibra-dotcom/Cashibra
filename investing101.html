Cashibra — Investing 101

Why Invest?

Investing helps your money grow faster than simply saving it. Over time, compounding and steady contributions can turn small amounts into significant sums.

Where can you invest?

Stocks: Own a tiny piece of a company — high risk, high return potential.
Mutual Funds: Many people pool money and a fund manager buys a set of stocks — good for beginners.
SIP (Systematic Investment Plan): Invest a fixed amount every month into a mutual fund — helps average out highs and lows.
ETFs: Like mutual funds but traded on stock exchanges.
