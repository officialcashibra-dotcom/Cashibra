What Are Digital Payments?

Digital payments are transactions made without physical cash. You use a phone, card, or online method to pay.

  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Debit / Credit Card
  • Net Banking
  • QR Code Scanning
  • Online Wallets
